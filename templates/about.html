About Us

From its inception, Tilottama College has relentlessly pursued excellence, dedicating itself to creating an educational environment that's not only comprehensive but also vibrant. Our mission is to unveil and foster the myriad potentials within every student, making us a true powerhouse of senior education in Nepal.

Introduction

Tilottama Campus was established in 1996 as the first private college in Rupandehi by a team of dedicated, experienced and enterprising academicians to cater for the growing demands of parents and students in higher education by providing quality education and guaranteeing top class graduates.

Since its establishment, the college has been running +2 programmes in science and management streams in affiliation with the National Education Board Nepal (NEB, Nepal). Similarly, it is also running Bachelor in Business Administration (BBA) programme in affiliation with Pokhara University. It is also planning to launch Master in Business Administration (MBA) in the near future.

The college has been imparting practical education. The college feels proud of the high success passing rate of students along with the university toppers for several years. The college graduates being placed at reputed organizations in senior-level positions justify the quality education and make us feel at the top.

The faculty of management of this college solely aims to inculcate quality education with an excellent state of the art facilities to the students who have chosen the pathway of management as their career or profession. This college places a high value on providing its students with a fulfilling educational experience in management disciplines. We are confident that you will find this college as the best platform to develop yourself.

Tilottama College, a prominent educational institution, takes pride in its prestigious standing and recognition. Nestled in the heart of the city, our college offers a tranquil and supportive environment, fostering the growth of young minds. Committed to providing top-tier education, we integrate cutting-edge pedagogical methods to ensure academic excellence, all while keeping the cost affordable for students.
